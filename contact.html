Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order? +

Simply click on the product you like on the Shop page, select WhatsApp or Email ordering, fill out your size, address, and quantity in the popup, and hit submit. Your order details will be pre-filled and sent to us immediately.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept Cash on Delivery (COD) across Bangladesh, as well as bKash, Nagad, and bank transfers for advance payments. We'll align payment details during our WhatsApp/Email chat.

How long does shipping take? +

Deliveries inside Dhaka usually take 24 to 48 hours. Deliveries outside Dhaka take 2 to 4 business days via courier services.
