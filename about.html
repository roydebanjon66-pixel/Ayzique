— Who We Are —

Defined By
Our Standard.

We craft high-end garments for those who value form, function, and absolute quality. No compromises.

Our Philosophy
"AYZIQUE is not just a brand name. It is a commitment to streetwear excellence and architectural silhouette design."

We believe streetwear should feel like luxury. By focusing on heavier fabrics, precise shoulder drops, and minimal graphics, we create pieces that last a lifetime. Every garment goes through months of fit testing and wash cycles before it earns the AYZIQUE mark.

Core Values

What We Stand For

01

Premium Craft

From 240+ GSM cotton fabrics to double-stitched hems, we construct garments designed to hold their shape and structure.

02

Perfect Silhouettes

We spent months perfecting our signature oversized drop-shoulder patterns. It sits perfectly on the body, offering modern drapes.

03

Ethical Sourcing

Every piece is locally manufactured in Bangladesh, supporting local craftsmen while maintaining premium export quality standards.

Milestones

The Journey

2023

The Spark

AYZIQUE was conceptualized by a group of designers frustrated by the lack of local options for high-quality minimalist streetwear in Bangladesh.

2024

First Prototype

After testing over 50 different fabric weights and fits, we launched our first signature drop shoulder t-shirt capsule, which sold out in days.

2025

Superstar Edition

Launched our limited Football Superstars Vintage collection featuring vintage wash mockups of football giants Neymar, Ronaldo, and Messi, redefining streetwear aesthetics.
